Quick help

Getting started — adding your clothes

Tap + on the Closet tab. You can Add Photo (snap or pick a picture — the AI tags the category, color, and fabric automatically), Import from Link (paste a product URL), or Add from Receipt (upload a receipt screenshot and every clothing item on it is added at once).

Auto-tagging didn't finish or got it wrong

If tagging fails you'll see an amber Retry badge on the item — tap it to try again. You can always edit any detail yourself by opening the item. Tagging needs an internet connection.

Signing in

Sign in with Apple, Google, or Email. With email, we send a 6-digit code (check spam if it doesn't arrive within a minute) — or choose the tap-to-sign-in link instead.

What's free, and what's Premium?

Free includes your core closet tools plus 25 AI stylist calls per month. Premium ($14.99/month or $49.99/year) unlocks unlimited AI styling (text), unlimited auto-tagging, cloud sync, and 30 Photo Studio image credits each month (plus a one-time welcome bundle when you sign up). Photo Studio credits power image features like photo cleanup — with virtual try-on coming soon. Need more? Premium members can top up: 20 credits for $4.99 or 60 for $9.99.

Managing or cancelling your subscription

Subscriptions are billed by Apple. To manage or cancel: open Settings on your device → tap your name → Subscriptions → WhichOutfit. Cancelling stops renewal but keeps Premium until the period ends.

Restoring purchases

Reinstalled or switched devices? Open the paywall (More → WhichOutfit Premium) and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in to the same account.

Redeeming an offer code

On the paywall, tap Redeem Code and enter your code in the Apple sheet that appears.

Referrals — 2 weeks of Premium + 15 free cleanups for both of you

Share your referral link with a friend. When they sign up, you both get 2 weeks of Premium plus 15 free cleanups. Look for Invite Friends in the app to grab your link.

Backup & sync (Premium)

With Premium, your closet, photos, worn log, saved looks, and style profile back up automatically and sync across your devices. Check status anytime in More → Backup & Sync — tap the row to sync on demand. Sign in with the same account on a new device and everything restores.

Deleting your account

Go to More → Delete Account. This permanently deletes your account and all server data (including backed-up photos) and can't be undone. An active subscription should be cancelled separately in Settings → Subscriptions, since billing is handled by Apple.

Privacy

Your photos stay on your device, with private cloud backup for Premium members. Read the full Privacy Policy.
